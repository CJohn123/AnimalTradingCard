Snow Leopards

<

Snow leopards are the only big cats the Asia desert home.

  • <Scientific Name: Pathera unica
  • Average Length: 39-51
  • Average Weight: 60-120
  • Average Lifespan: 12 years in the wild
  • Habitat: Cold & dry; Central Asia mountains

Medium-sized cats have thick and smoky fur coat & rosette spots patterns that blend well with the rocky mountains. Snow leopard's diet varies based on the availability of food, but they typically eat sheep and goat. Due to several years of prey and habitat loss & poaching, these majestic animals have become endangered.
